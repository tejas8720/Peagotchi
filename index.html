Why this project Exist?


Climate change may effect the production of crops, mostly affected by high temperature and water stress. Peagotchi is a project using block chain to quickly pinpoint the growth of Peacrop based on climate conditions. Dynamic NFT’s showcase the relationship of climate change with crop growth and yield stages using digital art. For interaction with user we build Peagotchiverse where different user can grow crops and see how Dynamic nft will work in metaverse. For more interative experience there will be Peagotchi Marketplace where they can buy cool stuff, crops or land for the Peagotchiverse.


Does Climatic Change really affect Crops?


Case Study or Instructions to grow crops in Peagotchi

  • Peas are cool season plant and thrives best at 12 – 18 C
  • The germination of seed takes place at 10C soil temperature
  • The plants that is shown in the mid of the March and harvested in middle 10 days of July
  • Plants are sensitive for frost conditions
  • From seeding emergence to harvest is 110 - 120 days
  • Each plant contains 8-10 pods and each pod contains 5-7 seeds
  • Light soils are preferred for cultivation
  • Seed germination is hampered when the temperature at the time of planting <5C
  • Peas can tolerate frost at the early stage of growth, but In later stages of crop growth causes considerable injury to newly opened flowers
